Spatial Analysis of robbery and theft crimes in Wuhan city

Team Leader

short description of the project

May,2019 ~ Jan,2020

Overview: This project is listed among A awards (highest level) of national college students' innovation and entrepreneurship program in 2019. The aim of this project is to provide a limited perspective on how geographical factors could potentially be related to the theft and robbery rate regionally in Wuhan City. Through utilizing knowledge and techniques from spatial statistics and geographical data processing, suggestions were given in order to improve the city's early warning system after analyzing the correlations between geographical factors and crime rate.
